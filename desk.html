Woodburned Desk

Woodburning, 2022

I was sick and tired of Ikea desks that never felt right and were always cracking and warping so I decided to make my own. I made the desk out of pine wood, and after it was to shape, I cut out logos and illustrations that relate to who I am as a person and decided to woodburn them directly on the side. These logos ranges from sports teams, video games, people, schools, towns, and much more.
