Jack Hughes Poster

Photoshop, 2022

Jack Hughes was a new breakout star (and he was on my fantasy hockey team!) when I first started getting into hockey, so he was one of my favorites. At the time, I played with transparency of fire and smoke in other projects and I spread it throughout the poster, making sure to circle him and highlight him by making a focus point. I also started downloading fonts for the first time so I downloaded a sports jersey font that matches the type on the back of the players jersey.
