Personal Info

  Hi! My name is Mason Gomes, a Graphic Design student at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth, and I focus on website design, as well as poster design. I’ve taken a handful of classes here that have a concentration in website design, such as Advanced UX/UI, Web Typography, and have taken multiple typography courses that helped me become more experienced with poster design. Within Graphic Design, I have experience in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign, as well as programs outside of Adobe such as Figma and VS Code. I am proficient in HTML and CSS, and know the fundamentals of JavaScript, and have coded two full portfolio websites. Here at UMass Dartmouth, I am a club officer of the UX/UI Club where we teach students the basics of HTML and CSS. As a student in the field of Graphic Design, I was always influenced by car color combinations and helmet designs in Formula 1 racing, and the hockey team logos and jerseys in the NHL, and this was a main reason why I became a graphic designer. For my career in the future, I aspire to be a website designer or a sports designer because both have played a significant role in my life. Making websites has always been a fun challenge and making posters has been with me since I first started as a graphic designer.
