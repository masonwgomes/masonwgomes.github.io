Formula 1 App Redesign

Figma, 2023

Formula 1 being my favorite sport to watch, I tend to use their mobile app to get quick info about an upcoming race weekend or basic info about some drivers. The app tends to be very frustrating to use and takes a lot buttons to get to certain info, so I redesigned it for a proejct. It was one of the most enjoyable things to make and being new to Figma at the time, it was very fun to make despite it being my first app-based project.
