100 Hockey Cards Poster

Illustrator and Photoshop, 2023

This project is based off 100 different hockey trading cards, just as the 100 Hockey Cards Website Mockup used. The requirement was to sort through them and create a poster based on the different categories of the cards. The viewer is to start looking at one of the circles on the rink, and locate the number of the corresponding player name, and looking at the circle percentage surround their flag nationality, be able to tell how long theyve been in the NHL and how old they are. Where the circle is located on the rink determines their position and if theyre left handed or right handed.
