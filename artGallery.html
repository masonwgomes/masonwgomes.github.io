Art Gallery Website

Figma, Leonardo.ai, ChatGPT, 2024

The goal of this website was to design a fake Art Gallery's website. While thinking about how I was going to make this, I thought of how I would make the "artwork" and I sure didn't want to make all of them. I recently heard my friend talking about a new Ai website that has image generation, so I decided to make the art gallery all by Ai. All art pieces and art artwork descriptions are Ai generated. Along with the Ai, I included a "Sign In" function that you have to go through first, and the info that you input is stored and displayed throughout the website.
